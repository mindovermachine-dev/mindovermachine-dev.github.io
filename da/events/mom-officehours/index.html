Mind over Machine — Office Hours

Every Tuesday 8:15 CET (1:00h)

Online (Slack Huddle)

Office Hours is a recurring event where we open up the floor for anyone to pitch an idea, ask for help, or just share something interesting.

Join the huddle on Slack

This is MoM’s weekly Office Hour meeting

The meeting is run as a huddle in our slack channel #mom-office-hours and is open to anyone who wants to join.

This meeting is an integrated part of our Community of Practice. We advocate full transparency and Legitimate Peripheral Participation. In short: no matter what you interest or incentive is: You are so welcome 🤩.

You should join if:

  • You have an idea you want to pitch and get feedback on
  • You want to ask for help on a project you are working on
  • You are already in one of the MoM circles and want to share your progress or get feedback
  • You don’t get what regenerative software is or why it matters — and want to learn more
  • You are just curious and don’t want anything in particular — just curious…

Lean Coffee style

The meeting is are run in a Lean Coffee style. This means that we will start with a blank agenda and the participants will propose topics to discuss. The topics will be voted on and the most popular ones will be discussed first. Each topic will have a timebox of 8 minutes and we will use a timer to keep track of time. If a topic is not finished within the timebox, we will vote on whether to continue or move on to the next topic.

If this is your first meeting

We have set up a few tools to support the logistices of the meeting. If this is your first time joining, we recommend that you do the following:

Prepare for the meeting

We have set up a few tools to support the logistices of the meeting, we recommend that you do the following beforehand

Step 1: Join our Slack

Create an account on our slack workspace. There is an open invitation waiting for you.

Create account on mindovermachine-dk.slack.com

Step 2: Visit and join the #mom-office-hours channel

The #mom-office-hours channel is dedicated to discussing the office hours. Also in-between scheduled meetings. If you join the channel you will also get notifications when the next office hour is started. Visit the channel and click the "Join channel" button.

Go to #mom-office-hours channel

Step 3: Join the Miro board "MoM Office Hours"

This is where we execute the Lean Coffee meeting. The board is open to all for viewing, but you need to join the board to get write access; an open invitation is waiting for you.

Join "MoM Office Hours" Miro board

Dont miss a beat - get the invites in your calendar!

We have set up a open google group office-hours.mindovermachine.dk which is invited to all office hour meetings. If you join the group, you will see the invites along with all your other calendar events. You can ignore or reject the invites if you don’t want to attend a specific meeting.

Join the open google group

Visit the group and click the “Join group” button. You will be asked to sign in with your google account and then you will be added to the group.

NOTE: Joining a google group requires a google account.

If you would like to get the invites set to an email address which is not a google account. You can contact us at hey@mindovermachine.dk.

Or you can help yourself: Simply ‘wrap’ on your current email address is a free google account. It’s harmless and non-intrusive, it simply allows you to use Google’s OAuth and services with any email address you have (not email obviously, but stuff like drive, photos, groups, gemini etc…).
