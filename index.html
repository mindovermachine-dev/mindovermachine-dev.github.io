Mind over Machine Regenerative software in practice

We are a non-profit foundation combining a think tank, a laboratory, and a community of practice. Our mission is to explore and share regenerative approaches to software development that create a net positive long-term value for people and the planet.

What we do

Three pillars of our work

Think tank: Thought leadership

We devlop, analyse, reserch and publish open end free materials about regenerative software development, system thinking, ethics, and long-term healthy development practices.
